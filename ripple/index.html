This Privacy Policy explains how the Ripple Android app ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. The short version: the app collects no personal data and sends nothing off your device.

1. The short version

Ripple is built to be private by design. It runs entirely on your device. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and the app makes no network or internet connections. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or usage data with anyone.

2. Data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal data.

The app does store the information you enter and generate while using it — such as your hydration goal, body weight (if you choose to enter it for goal calculation), drink logs, movement-break logs, reminder settings, streaks, and app preferences. All of this is stored locally on your device only, using Android's on-device storage (a Room/SQLite database and DataStore preferences).

This on-device data is never uploaded, never sent to us or any third party, and is not accessible to us. It exists only on your phone.

3. No network, no analytics, no third parties

  • The app makes no internet or network requests.
  • The app contains no analytics or telemetry SDKs.
  • The app contains no advertising and no ad SDKs.
  • The app contains no third-party tracking libraries or SDKs.
  • The app does not integrate with cloud services, Google Fit, Health Connect, social networks, or any external account.

4. Permissions and why they are used

The app requests only the permissions needed to deliver reliable reminders on your device:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): to show your water and movement reminders.
  • Exact alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM): to fire reminders at the right time, even under battery optimisation (Doze).
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): to re-schedule your reminders after your device restarts, so reminders keep working after a reboot.
  • Vibrate (VIBRATE): to optionally vibrate for reminders.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data. The app has no internet permission.

5. Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. No data is collected or transmitted regardless of the user's age.

6. How to access, export, or delete your data

Because all data lives only on your device:

  • Access: your data is visible to you directly inside the app.
  • Deletion: clearing the app's storage (Android Settings → Apps → Ripple → Storage → Clear data) erases your data, and uninstalling the app removes all of its data from your device. There is nothing stored on our side to delete, because we never receive your data.

7. Data security

Your data remains within the app's private storage area on your device, protected by Android's standard app sandboxing. Because the app never transmits data over a network, there is no data "in transit" to intercept.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the revised policy at the same URL where this document is hosted. Material changes will also be reflected in the app's store listing.
