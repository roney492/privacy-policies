# Privacy Policy — Luna: Period & Cycle Tracker

**Last updated: 21 June 2026**

Luna ("the app", "we") is a period and menstrual cycle tracker developed by MTracker.
Your privacy is the foundation of this app. This policy explains, plainly, what
happens to your information.

## The short version

**Luna does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal or health data.**
Everything you enter stays on your device.

## What data the app stores

When you use Luna you may enter:

- Period start and end dates
- Cycle, period, and luteal phase length settings
- Daily logs: flow intensity, moods, symptoms, and free-text notes
- Reminder preferences

All of this is stored **locally on your device only**, inside the app's private
storage (an on-device database and preferences file). It is never uploaded to us or
to any third party.

## What we do NOT do

- We do **not** have servers that receive your data.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- We do **not** request the Internet permission — the app has no network access.
- We do **not** sell or share your data with anyone.
- We do **not** require an account, email, or sign-in.

## Permissions

- **Notifications** — used only to show your local period and fertile-window
  reminders on your device. You can disable these at any time.
- **Run at startup / boot completed** — used only to re-schedule your local
  reminders after the device restarts.

## Device backups

If you have enabled Android's own device backup or device-transfer features, your
on-device app data may be included in those system backups, which are governed by
Google's and your device manufacturer's policies — not by Luna.

## Deleting your data

You are always in control. Use **Settings → Clear all data** to permanently delete
every period and daily log from your device. Uninstalling the app also removes all
of its data.

## Children

Luna is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children.

## Medical disclaimer

Luna is a general wellness and educational tool. It is **not** a medical device and
**not** a contraceptive. Predictions are estimates based on your input and should not
be relied upon to prevent or achieve pregnancy. Consult a qualified healthcare
professional for medical advice.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
