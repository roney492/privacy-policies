# Age of Power — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 14, 2026_

This policy explains, in plain English, what Age of Power does and does not
do with your information. Age of Power is a single-player strategy game for
Android.

If you only read one section, read this one: **Age of Power runs entirely on
your device. We do not operate servers that store your data. We do not show
ads. There are no in-app purchases, and the game does not make network
requests during play.**

---

## 1. Who we are

Age of Power is created and published by **Roney Dsilva**, an independent
developer. Roney is the person responsible for your data under this policy
(the "data controller"). You can get in touch any time at
**roney4972@gmail.com**.

The Android application ID is `com.ageofpower.app`.

---

## 2. What we collect

We collect the **minimum** needed for the game to work, and it all stays on
your device:

- **Game saves and settings** — your match progress (Age, resources, your
  base, the world state) and preferences (audio volume, camera rotation
  on/off, etc.). Stored locally in the app's private storage.
- **Anonymous crash logs** — if the app crashes, Android may send a technical
  stack trace to Google Play Console so we can fix the bug. These logs are
  technical and contain no identifier that we set. You can disable this in
  Android Settings (search for "Usage & diagnostics").

That is the entire list.

---

## 3. What we do NOT collect

We want to be explicit. Age of Power does **not** collect or transmit any of
the following:

- Android Advertising ID (AAID)
- Device serial number, IMEI, MAC address, or SIM serial
- Precise or coarse location
- Contacts, photos, microphone audio, or camera images
- Biometric data (fingerprint, face geometry, voice prints)
- Account identifiers (Google account, email address)
- IP address (the game does not make network requests in this version)
- Browsing history or your usage of other apps

We also do not show ads, do not include any third-party analytics SDK, and do
not include any social media SDK.

---

## 4. Where data is stored

- **On your device.** All save and settings data lives in the app's private,
  sandboxed storage on your phone or tablet. Only Age of Power can read it.
- **Optionally, in your Google Drive.** If you have Android Auto Backup turned
  on for your device (it is on by default), Android may copy app data to **your
  own Google Drive backup** so it can be restored if you switch phones. That
  backup belongs to you, not us — we never see it. You control it from
  Settings → Google → Backup, and you can delete it any time.
- **Nowhere else.** We do not operate servers that store your data.

---

## 5. How long we keep it

Age of Power is an offline, on-device app. The only data that exists is on your
device, and it stays there only as long as it serves your gameplay:

| Item | Purpose | Kept until |
|---|---|---|
| Game saves | Resume your match where you left off. | You delete the save in-app, clear app data, or uninstall the app. |
| Settings | Remember your preferences. | Same as above. |
| Crash diagnostics | Let us fix bugs. Stack traces only; no identifier we set. | Held by Google Play's crash pipeline per Google's retention; we keep no separate copy. |

To delete everything: clear the app's data in Android Settings → Apps → Age of
Power → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app. If you also want to remove
the off-device copy, delete the Auto Backup in your Google Drive.

We do not have a server-side delete button because we do not have a server.

---

## 6. Third parties

For this version of Age of Power there are no third parties embedded in the
game. Specifically:

- No ad network.
- No analytics SDK.
- No A/B testing SDK.
- No social login.
- No push notification service.

The only third party in the picture is **Google**, because the app is
distributed through Google Play and Google's crash-reporting pipeline runs at
the operating-system level. We do not control Google's data practices; see
Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

If we ever add a third-party SDK, we will update this policy **before**
shipping that update.

---

## 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete,
export, or restrict the processing of your personal data. Because Age of Power
stores everything on your device and we hold nothing on a server, you exercise
all of these directly:

- **Access / export:** your data is the save and settings files on your device.
- **Delete:** clear the app's data or uninstall the app (section 5).
- **Restrict / object:** the game collects nothing beyond section 2; there is
  nothing additional to restrict.
- **Contact us:** email **roney4972@gmail.com**. We respond within 30 days.

We do not sell or share personal information, and we do not use it for targeted
advertising.

---

## 8. Children

Age of Power is a general-audience strategy game and is not directed at
children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If
you believe a child has provided us information, contact roney4972@gmail.com and
we will address it. Because the game stores data only on the device, clearing
the app's data removes it.

---

We will update the "Last updated" date at the top whenever this policy changes.
Material changes (a new third party, a new data category, a change in
retention) will be reflected here before the corresponding app update ships.
